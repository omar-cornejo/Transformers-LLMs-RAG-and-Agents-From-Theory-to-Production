# Beast

> "Gohan, listen carefully! You have the potential to become the most powerful being on Earth. You have to believe in yourself and unleash that power! It's life or death, so draw out all your strength and use it to protect your world and everyone on it!"
> — *Orange Piccolo's motivational speech to Gohan in Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero.*

**Beast** (ビースト, *Bīsuto*) is a powerful transformation used by Gohan. The name was given to him by Piccolo. This form is unique to Gohan (outside of avatars in video games such as *Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2*) and, when he uses it, he is officially known as **Gohan Beast**.

* **Alternative names:** Beast Mode, Saigo Gohan, Final Gohan, Beast Gohan, Super Awakening: Beast, Last Gohan, End Gohan, Beast Awoken.
* **Debut:**

    * **Manga:** Chapter *"Son Gohan's Ultimate Awakening!"*
    * **Video Games:** *Dragon Ball Heroes*
* **Known Users:** Gohan, Future Warrior (*Xenoverse 2*).
* **Class:** Power Up, Transformation.
* **Similar Techniques/Forms:** Orange Piccolo, Super Saiyan 2, Super Saiyan 3, Super Saiyan Rage, Perfected Ultra Instinct, Super Full Power, Ultra Ego, Legendary Super Saiyan.

## Concept and Creation

Akira Toriyama explained that the name comes from the feeling that "the wild beast inside him has awakened." The initial design featured paler skin and a more intimidating face, but considering it wasn't enough, Toriyama decided to add the massive, spiky hair.

Before its name was finalized, this form appeared in the storyboards as **Final Gohan**. Thematically, the transformation fulfills Gohan's goal of evolving into "a definitive form that no one has ever seen before" and growing as his own person (or as a human), rather than solely following the evolutionary path of the Saiyans.

---

## Appearance

The beginning of the transformation visually mimics Gohan's first awakening into Super Saiyan 2 against Cell.

* **Eyes:** They flicker from black to red before manifesting the full form. Once completed, the eyes turn completely red with black pupils.
* **Hair and Face:** The eyebrows turn white. The hair takes on a light gray tone (similar to Perfected Ultra Instinct or a Supreme Kai's), spiking and lengthening sharply backward with a large frontal lock falling to the nose.
* **Aura:** It maintains a white-bluish glow surrounded by a magenta-reddish inner aura, wrapped in turn by magenta lightning. His *ki* erupts with the intensity of a massive geyser.
* **Voice:** In the English dub (Funimation), his voice becomes notably deeper and more menacing (similar to Super Saiyan 4). When transforming for the first time, an echo of his childlike Super Saiyan 2 scream can be heard superimposed over the current one.

---

## Usage and Power

Gohan Beast is a direct evolution of his Ultimate state (*Ultimate Gohan*) and is currently his most powerful transformation.

* **Raw Power:** In the movie, he defeated a weakened Cell Max with total ease. In the manga, it costs him a bit more effort, but he manages to overwhelm him after a brief clash of energies, destroying his core with a *Special Beam Cannon* (Makankosappo).
* **Comparison with Goku and Vegeta:** Bulma speculated that this form could make Gohan stronger than Goku and Vegeta. Later, on Beerus' planet, Gohan Beast manages to match Goku in combat using *Perfected Ultra Instinct*. Gohan demonstrated superior raw strength and speed (managing to knock Goku out of his state temporarily), which Goku countered with autonomous evasion and his greater martial arts experience.
* **Candidate for God of Destruction:** Whis noted that this transformation is not divine in nature, but a product of Gohan's unique potential. His power level led Whis to suggest Gohan as a God of Destruction, but Beerus rejected it, arguing that he does not have the right personality.
* **Control and Disadvantages:** Thanks to his training with Piccolo, Gohan learned to access the form at will. However, if his anger overflows, he runs the risk of losing control and going *berserk*. His current strategy is to fight just on the edge of losing control to maximize his power, which drains his energy and stamina extremely quickly.

## Trivia

* This transformation bears a certain aesthetic resemblance to the way Super Saiyan is drawn with limited colors in the original *Dragon Ball* manga.
* Promotional art for Gohan Beast shows him in the exact same pose as Goku when he first transformed into a Super Saiyan on Namek.
* In the manga chapter *"Son Goku vs. Son Gohan"*, the form was drawn in one panel without pupils, but it was confirmed to be an art error.
* When Vegeta uses Super Saiyan 3 as an adult in *Dragon Ball Daima*, his hair lengthens but remains spiky, giving him a silhouette very similar to Gohan Beast.
